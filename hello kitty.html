Hello Kitty


Facts

  • Her full name is Kitty White
  • She is little girl (and not a cat)
  • She was born in the suburbs of London, England, where she still lives with her parents
  • She has a twin sister named Mimmy, who wears a yellow bow on her right ear
  • Kitty's hobbies are traveling, reading and baking
  • The Japanese company Sanrio first created Hello Kitty in 1974. A year later, her image, including her signature red bow, was introduced to the public on a small children's coin purse that sold for less than a dollar

 A Paragraph about Hello Kitty 

 Hello Kitty is a popular character created by the Japanese company Sanrio. She is a white 
 cat with a red bow on her head and no mouth. Hello Kitty was first introduced in 1974 and 
 has since become a global phenomenon, appearing on a wide range of products from school 
 supplies to clothing. Despite her lack of a mouth, Hello Kitty is known for her positive 
 and friendly personality, making her a beloved character among people of all ages. Her 
 simple design and cute appearance have made her a timeless icon in popular culture.
