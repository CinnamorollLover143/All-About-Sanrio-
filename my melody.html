My Melody


Facts About My Melody

  • Japanese name: マイメロディ(pronounced Mai Merodi)
  • Nickname: My Melo.
  • Birthday: January 18.
  • Gender: Girl.
  • Species: Rabbit.
  • Zodiac sign: Capricorn.
  • Hobby: Baking cookies and almond cake.
  • Fun Fact: My Melody had no mouth in Hello Kitty's TV series.

Here are some interesting facts about My Melody

A Sanrio character who was first introduced in 1975

Little Red Riding Hood
My Melody was originally a rabbit version of Little Red Riding Hood, and she always wears a hood as a keepsake from her grandmother.
Family
My Melody lives in the magical forest of Mariland with her rabbit parents and loves spending time with her family. She also has a younger brother named Rhythm.
Friendships
My Melody is best friends with Hello Kitty, who is only one year older than her and is also from London. She also has a friend named Flat, a little mouse.
Hobbies
My Melody enjoys baking cookies with her mother and her favorite food is almond pound cake.
Anime series
My Melody was the inspiration for the Japanese anime series Please My Melody, which ran from 2005 to 2006.

To Sum it all Up 

   My Melody is a popular Sanrio character known for her cute appearance and sweet 
 personality. She is a white rabbit with long ears and a red hood on her head. My Melody 
 loves to bake and spend time with her friends, always bringing happiness wherever she 
 goes. She is often seen with her best friend, a mouse named Flat. My Melody's positive 
 attitude and caring nature make her a beloved character among fans of Sanrio.
