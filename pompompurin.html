Pompompurin


Facts

  • Pompompurin is a little yellow dog
  • Pompompurin is good at napping, eating, and something he calls "Purin Aerobic
  • He loves soft things, especially pudding, and cream caramel pudding is his favorite
  • He also likes to collect shoes, especially his owner-mother's sandals and his owner-father's leather shoes
  • Pompompurin is friendly and can make friends easily
  • His favorite phrase is "Let's go out!" and his least-liked word is "stay"
  • Pompompurin always wears a little brown beret on top of his head
  • Pompompurin's friends include Muffin the hamster, Scone the mouse, Bagel the squirrel, and Custard the bird.

All About Pompompurin 

 Pompompurin is a popular Sanrio character that is loved by many people around the world.
 This cute golden retriever has a pudding-like appearance and is often seen wearing a 
 signature brown beret. Pompompurin is known for being friendly, kind, and always ready to 
 help others. Fans of this adorable character can find merchandise, toys, and even themed cafes 
 dedicated to Pompompurin. Overall, Pompompurin brings joy and happiness to those who admire and 
 appreciate its lovable charm.
